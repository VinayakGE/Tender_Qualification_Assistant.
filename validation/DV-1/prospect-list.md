## Shortlist Criteria

A prospect moves from Research → Shortlisted when:

1. Specialization covers sectors that RA-2.5 identifies as weakest (fill in after RA-2.5)
2. Est. client count ≥ 10 (enough decisions to observe a pattern, not a single-company bid manager)
3. Evidence Potential = High (multi-sector portfolio OR known for cross-authority work)
4. LinkedIn or direct website contact available (cold outreach possible without intermediary)

## Session Prep Protocol (for when outreach begins)

Before each confirmed session:

1. Ask the consultant for 2–3 tenders they evaluated recently (redact confidential details if needed).
2. Run those tenders through the engine before the meeting.
3. Record engine output privately — do not share before the session.
4. During the session: let them walk through their reasoning first.
5. After they conclude: show engine output, compare step by step.

Record per session: Agreement / Divergence class / New signals surfaced / Trust observations.
